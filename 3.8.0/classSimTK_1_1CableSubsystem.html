Detailed Description

This subsystem manages cables spanning between two points in a system.

Each cable is represented by a CableSpan. Obstacles can be added to the cable, and must be wrapped over. The calculated path will consist of a series of straight line segments between obstacles, and curved segments (geodesics) over the obstacles. Force elements defined elsewhere may make use of the cables to apply forces to the system.

See CableSpan.
